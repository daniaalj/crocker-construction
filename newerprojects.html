The Lift Wellness Center

Crocker Construction constructed this $17 million project in early 2013. The 82,000 sq. ft. facility features

  • a 64,000 sq. ft. fitness center
  • a 14,000 sq. ft. clinic with 16 examination rooms
  • a 4,000 sq. ft. health education room with multi-use classrooms
American Planning Association Tennessee Award American Institute of Architects Memphis Award Crocker Construction was awarded the Golden Aurora Award for Commercial Project Healthcare Facility.

Jackson Central Merry High School

Crocker Construction recently did extensive renovations for JCM. The $17.12 million project was constructed in 2021 and covers 132,870 sq. ft. It has 29 classrooms, band and choir rooms, and a brand-new lobby that showcases the school's vibrant history. In addition to the renovations, a new field house with a 1,250-seat gymnasium was built.

Madison Academic High School

Crocker Construction built the new three-story Madison Academic High School which was a $14.87 million project consisting of 58,800 sf and 27 classrooms. It has a monumental staircase, trophy cases, 4 laboratories, and band and theater rooms.
